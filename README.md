# IBM MAS Training By Master System Infotama

## Maximo Application Suite
IBM Maximo, part of the IBM Maximo Application Suite (MAS), is widely recognized as one of the top-performing Enterprise Asset Management (EAM) platforms. According to the Verdantix Green Quadrant for EAM Software 2024, IBM Maximo was positioned as a Leader, highlighting its innovation, robust functionality, and industry relevance
Starting from version 8, IBM restructured its offering under the Maximo Application Suite (MAS), where Maximo Manage became the new identity of the traditional EAM core. MAS is designed as a modular, integrated platform that delivers end-to-end asset performance management on a unified architectur

## APCO
APCO, short for Agile Product Collaboration, is a concept that emerged from the idea of providing comprehensive, real-time information based on data captured from sensors on individual devices. This information is displayed visually with 3D views. This capability is possible due to APCO’s integration of advanced technology from leading global companies, including IBM, Autodesk, and AWS.

## Training Overview
This training is designed to provide a deep understanding of the Install  of IBM Maximo Application Suite (MAS) 9.x in asset lifecycle management, maintenance digitalization, and operational optimization. Participants will learn how to manage asset data (Asset Registry), build efficient maintenance workflows (Work Management), and utilize Preventive Maintenance features to prevent equipment breakdowns. The training covers both theory and hands-on practice, focusing on navigating the new MAS 9.0 technical perspective for IT Division.

## Training Objective
●	Understand the fundamental concepts of MAS 9.1 as well as the new architecture and navigation within the MAS Manage environment.
●	Capable of building and setup MAS 9.1.
●	Understanding MAS Mobile.
●	Understanding MAS integration thru Rest API.


## Training Prerequisites
●	Basic knowledge of maintenance Maximo EAM version 7.6.1.
●	Basic knowledge of RedHat Openshift
●	Stable internet or corporate intranet access to connect to the IBM Maximo training server.
●	Basic experience with CMMS (Computerized Maintenance Management System) or other ERP applications is highly beneficial.
